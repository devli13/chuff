Voted Best Deli by The Daily Cal Since 1996.

Cheese n' Stuff's cheerful, speedy and experienced staff have a history of ensuring that Berkeley's college community is satisfied.

It's about schmoozing with Sam Juha, the store's owner whom everyone knows on a first-name basis - and who in turn seems to know everyone who comes in. Truth is, Sam basically knows every UC Berkeley Advocate, Gamer, Jock, ASUC Leader and Frat Boy.

No one in the community - not even those who have bled the Berkeley spirit through their tie-dyed T-shirts for several lifetimes now - can protest our great prices. Just $4.95 will score you a sandwich loaded with meat, cheese and the rest of the trimmings, served on your choice of fresh bread.
